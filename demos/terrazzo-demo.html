Components — Slab Applications

Applications

Flooring Systems

Poured-in-place terrazzo for continuous monolithic surfaces. Embedding brass divider strips allows for expansion joints and multi-color fields.

Thickness: 38mm minimum

Countertops

Pre-cast slabs for kitchens and bars. Higher aggregate-to-matrix ratio reveals larger stone chips, celebrating the material's composite nature.

Finish: Polished to 3000 grit

Wall Cladding

Vertical applications using epoxy-based matrix. Lighter than cement terrazzo, allowing thinner sections for facade and interior walls.

Panel size: up to 3m × 1.5m

Furniture

Tables, benches, and pedestals cast from offcuts. The philosophy of repurposing waste extends from aggregate to finished form.

Zero-waste production

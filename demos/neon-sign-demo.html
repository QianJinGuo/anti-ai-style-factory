Lighting Fixtures

Bent Tube

Hand-bent glass tubing filled with noble gas. Each curve shaped over an open flame. Imperfections are the signature of the artisan.

Transformer

High-voltage core-and-coil transformer. Steps 120V up to 9kV–15kV to ionize the gas. The hum is part of the atmosphere.

Glow Effect

Light achieved through diffusion, not shadow. box-shadow with large blur radii simulates gas scattering in darkness.

Flicker Script

Realistic neon flicker uses irregular keyframes — a dying transformer doesn't strobe evenly. The imperfection sells the illusion.

Interface Components

Card Structure

This card utilizes a 1px border to define space without the use of shadows. The background is pure white to contrast slightly against the Washi paper background.

Typography Hierarchy

Headings are tight and dark. Body text is widely spaced (1.8 line-height) to create "Ma" (negative space) between lines of text, improving legibility and calm.

Interactive States

Hover states are subtle. Backgrounds invert or shift to Dust Beige. No animations or glows are used. Interaction is functional and immediate.

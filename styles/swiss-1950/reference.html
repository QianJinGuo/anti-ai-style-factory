Principles

Mathematical Grid

The grid system determines the position of all elements. No exceptions. Alignment is objective, not subjective.

Objective Typography

Helvetica in all weights. The weight contrast carries hierarchy. No decorative faces, no serifs for emphasis.

White Space as Structure

Generous margins between sections. The empty space is not wasted — it communicates hierarchy and breathing room.

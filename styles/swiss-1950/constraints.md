# 瑞士国际主义 — Anti-AI Constraints

These constraints apply ON TOP of the 6 golden rules.

## Style-specific rules

1. **Typography** — Helvetica/Neue Haas
2. **Color discipline** — Derived from 瑞士国际主义 palette only
3. **Spatial logic** — Follow 网格系统；无衬线体；数学化排版

## Forbidden (beyond 6 golden rules)

- Any visual element not justified by the 瑞士国际主义 design principle
- Decorative elements that don't serve the content structure

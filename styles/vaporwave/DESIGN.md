## Layout

Layouts are grid-based but intentionally broken.

1.  **Grid System:** Use a strict 8px or 16px grid, but allow elements to overflow or misalign slightly.
2.  **Chessboard Backgrounds:** Use a subtle checkerboard pattern (`#1A1A2E` and `#252540`) for main sections to evoke digital space.
3.  **Asymmetry:** Place classical busts or statues off-center, overlapping text or breaking container bounds.
4.  **Whitespace:** Use generous padding, but fill it with noise, scanlines, or repeating geometric patterns.

## Elevation & Depth

Vaporwave rejects soft shadows. Depth is achieved through:

1.  **Hard Shadows:** Use `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #FF71CE;` for cards and buttons. No blur.
2.  **Layering:** Overlap elements with low-opacity PNGs of marble textures, palm trees, or Roman columns.
3.  **Scanlines:** Add a horizontal line pattern overlay to the entire page to simulate CRT monitors.
